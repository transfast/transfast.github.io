Packaging service

Transfast Shipping Ltdoffers professional packing and unpacking services 7 days a week. We can provide either competitive hourly rates or fixed price quotes for our packing services, we can also arrange the collection of your packing boxes and materials upon requestAll glassware and crockery is carefully wrapped in paper and placed into reinforced cardboard boxes. Art collections, paintings, statues, antique furniture and mirrors will be bubble wrapped and also wrapped with blankets to ensure a safe and secure move. All clothes on hangers will be placed into wardrobe boxes, to remain crease free. All other items will be neatly and carefully placed into our removal grade boxes, and clearly labelled with details of contents and destination rooms..

Storage

We can help you move all your furniture and possessions into storage as we work closely with several storage facilities throughout USA. All the storage units are fully alarmed and provide a CCTV monitored storage area making sure you are in safe hands when choosing us to store your possessions. Most storage units are able to take the contents of an average size living room, so 25 boxes and 3 or 4 large pieces of furniture.
